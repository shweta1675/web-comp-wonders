Explore the 7 Wonders

Great Wall of China

The Great Wall stretches over 13,000 miles and was built to protect Chinese states from invasions.

Petra, Jordan

Petra is an ancient rock-cut city known for its pink sandstone cliffs and water conduit system.

Christ the Redeemer

Standing atop Corcovado mountain, this 98-ft tall statue overlooks Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.

Machu Picchu, Peru

This Incan citadel in the Andes mountains remains one of the world's greatest archaeological sites.

Chichen Itza, Mexico

An ancient Mayan city famous for its large pyramid, El Castillo, and astronomical significance.

Roman Colosseum

This Roman amphitheatre could hold 50,000 spectators and hosted gladiator battles and dramas.

Taj Mahal, India

A white marble mausoleum built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.
